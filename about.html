Who We Are

A Unified Approach

Pivot Aide is not a generalist firm. We are a corporate financial advisory built for businesses that have outgrown basic bookkeeping—combining tax advisory, ledger precision, and enterprise ERP consulting into one connected system designed to scale with you.

Our work begins where most accountants stop. We align tax strategy with ledger accuracy, then engineer the ERP infrastructure to make both sustainable—customized workflows, automated reconciliation, and reporting architectures built around your specific industry, not a generic template. Whether you are consolidating multi-entity operations, preparing for a funding round, or expanding into new markets, we build the financial backbone that makes execution possible.

From our offices in Hyattsville, Maryland, and Virginia, we partner with ambitious leadership teams who understand that financial systems are not a back-office function—they are a competitive advantage. We don't hand you a report and walk away. We embed into your operations, align with your growth strategy, and build systems that compound in value the longer we work together.

Our commitment is absolute transparency, deep collaboration, and measurable business outcomes. Every engagement is built on the conviction that the right financial infrastructure—precisely configured, strategically maintained—is the foundation every high-growth business deserves.
