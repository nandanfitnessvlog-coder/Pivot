Underwriting Standard

What Lenders Require

Lenders primarily evaluate whether a business generates sufficient net cash flow to comfortably repay borrowed funds. The core metric used is the Debt Service Coverage Ratio (DSCR), calculated as Net Operating Income divided by Total Debt Service.

Weak, incomplete, or unreconciled financial records frequently trigger notice delays or application rejections. Establishing professional monthly bookkeeping and structured ledger files is the single most effective way to strengthen funding readiness.

  • ✓ Accrual-basis bookkeeping and ledger clarity
  • ✓ Reconciled balance sheets matching bank registers
  • ✓ Documented cash flow histories and scenario models
